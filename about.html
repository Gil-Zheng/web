About Badminton

Badminton is a indoor sport played with a racket and a shuttlecock. The sport dates back to Ancient Greece, China, and India. The shuttlecock is a ball covered in feathers so there is more drag. It is played on a court with the dimensions of 44 feet by 17 feet and a 5 feet net. The goal is to hit the shuttlecock onto the desinated area on the other side of the net. The sport has been in the Summer Olympics since 1992. Origins of Badminton; Brittanica
