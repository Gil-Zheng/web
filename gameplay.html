Scoring and Gameplay

The game starts off with an coin flip. Whoever calls the right side gets to serve first. The two teams play a total of three games and have to reach a score of 21 inorder to win. If the score is tied 20-20, the first team to get ahead two points win. If it continues to tie, the first to 30 wins. Each team switch sides between every game. In order to win, you need to win 2 out of 3 three games. Game of Badminton Article
