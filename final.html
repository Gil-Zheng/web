Petinator

A game created to guess your breed of cat or dog based on their characteristics and traits.

Created by Kevin Liu and Gil Zheng (Period 4) to demostrate the many tools of Python. Specifically using lists, loops, recursion, if-statements, and inputs. Also utilizing CVS files for sorting data and indexing.
